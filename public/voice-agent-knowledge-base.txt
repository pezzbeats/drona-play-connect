SECTION 4 — PAYMENT / PAYMENT KAISE KAREIN
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Q: Payment ke kya options hain?
A: Teen options hain:
1. Razorpay — Online payment gateway. Cards, UPI, NetBanking, Wallets sab accept karta hai. Sabse fast option hai — ticket automatic generate ho jaata hai.
2. UPI QR — Hotel ka QR code scan karo aur payment karo. Screenshot upload karo verification ke liye.
3. Pay at Hotel — Hotel aakaar cash ya card se pay karo. Tickets tab generate honge jab admin payment verify kare.

Q: Razorpay se payment kaise hoti hai?
A: Registration ke Step 3 mein "Pay via Razorpay" select karo. Ek summary page aayegi jo aapka order confirm karega — name, seats, price sab dikhega. Confirm karne ke baad Razorpay ka payment window khulega. Payment complete hone ke baad aapke tickets automatically generate ho jaate hain — koi screenshot upload karne ki zaroorat nahi!

Q: UPI se payment kaise karein?
A: Step 3 mein "UPI QR" select karo. Hotel ka QR code scan karo, apni amount daal ke pay karo, phir payment ka screenshot upload karo. Hamare team verification ke baad aapki tickets generate kar denge.

Q: Pay at Hotel kaise kaam karta hai?
A: Agar aap online payment nahi karna chahte, toh "Pay at Hotel" select karein. Aapki booking save ho jaayegi. Hotel pahunche, counter par apna naam aur mobile batayein aur cash ya card se pay karein. Tickets tab issue ki jaayengi.

Q: Mera payment fail ho gaya — kya karein?
A: Ghabrao mat! Agar Razorpay payment fail ho gayi hai toh:
- "Retry Razorpay Payment" button dabao — booking safe hai, dobara try ho sakta hai
- Ya UPI QR select karke alternative se pay karo
- Ya hotel aa jaao aur counter par pay karo
Agar paisa cut gaya lekin ticket nahi mili, 24 ghante ke andar call karein: +91 72170 16170 — Razorpay payment reference number ready rakhein.

Q: Payment cancel kar di — ab kya hoga?
A: Booking save hai, payment cancel thi toh koi charge nahi hua. Wapas aao website par, apna mobile number daalein tickets check karne ke section mein — wahan se retry kar sakte hain ya koi aur payment method choose kar sakte hain.

Q: Duplicate payment ho gayi — kya hoga?
A: Agar same order ke liye do baar payment cut gayi hai, toh hamare team ko batao. Duplicate payment 5 business days mein original payment instrument par wapas aa jaayegi.

Q: Kya payment secure hai?
A: Haan, poori tarah secure hai. Razorpay India ka trusted payment gateway hai aur PCI DSS compliant hai. Hotel ka UPI payment bhi secure hai. Aapki financial details hamare server par store nahi hoti.

Q: Payment proof ka screenshot kab upload karna hota hai?
A: Sirf UPI QR wale option mein screenshot upload karna hota hai. Razorpay se payment karne par screenshot ki koi zaroorat nahi — automatic verification ho jaati hai.
